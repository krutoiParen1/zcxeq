ГЛАВНЫЕ МАКАКИ

Etienne

Etienne is a Hero in Bloons TD 6, released in the Version 20.0 update. He is a drone operator who launches flying attack drones. He initially has 55 range with his main attack being a ranged attack from his drone.

Benjamin

Benjamin is a Hero who first appears in the Bloons TD 6 Generation, starting with Bloons TD 6. He is the fifth character to be released for that game generation. He has since subsequently appeared in later games.

Quincy

Quincy is a Hero in the game Bloons TD 6. He specializes in archery-related weaponry. His main attack is an arrow that ricochets off of 3 targets. Quincy has medium range. He is an all-around Hero with a decent attack speed, good against.

Sauda

Sauda is a Hero in Bloons TD 6, first released in Version 24.0. She is a swordmaster, and is the second melee hero in the game (the first being Pat Fusty). She uses her two swords to pop bloons, dealing one damage per attack across a 90-180 degree cone.

Pat Fusty

Pat Fusty is a hero in Bloons TD 6 who was added in the game in the 9.0 update. He is a gigantic monkey who uses slam attacks to crush Bloons. At level 1, his slams each hit a single bloon and create a blast that hits up to 10 other bloons nearby.

Ezili 

Ezili is a Hero who first appears in the Bloons TD 6 Generation, starting with Bloons TD 6. She is the seventh character to be released for that game generation, first added in Version 7.0 for Bloons TD 6. She has since subsequently appeared in later games.
